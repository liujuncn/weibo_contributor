Sina weibo content contributor ecosystem

This chart visualize current contributor and community ecosystem of the most popular content on Sina weibo(Chinese twitter). The source data are TOP 10k microblogs of the day according to their propagation index, sampled every wednesday from 2013/08/07 to 2013/11/06. It reflects that most active(influence) communitis and users on the platform. I think the result will helpful for sociologist, community operator and digital marketer. The bubble size representatives how much content they produce(except the larggest one). In community view, you can click one community bubble to view the users detail in it.
